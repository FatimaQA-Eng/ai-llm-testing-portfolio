## About This Portfolio
This repository documents my hands-on exploration of testing AI-powered 
and LLM-based applications using structured QA techniques.

With 10+ years of manual QA experience across healthcare, finance, and SaaS, 
I apply critical thinking and test design skills to evaluate AI systems for 
accuracy, safety, consistency, and reliability.

---

## Folders

| Folder | What I Tested |
|--------|--------------|
| `hallucination-testing` | Asked AI factual questions and documented where it gave confident but wrong or unverifiable answers |
| `adversarial-testing` | Used tricky, manipulative, and edge-case inputs to test AI safety and robustness |
| `context-memory-testing` | Verified whether AI correctly retains and updates information across a conversation |
| `ai-test-case-generation` | Used AI to generate test cases from requirements, then compared against my own manual analysis |
